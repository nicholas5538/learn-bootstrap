About Us

We are the largest seller of tofu in Singapore and, as a business generates annual sales of S$8m.

Our History

2008

Unicurd was acquired by Vitasoy International Holdings Limited (VIHL) through its subsidiary Vitasory Investment (Singapore) Pte. Limited.

1996

The company moved into its newly constructed 2700 square meter factory in Senoko, with over S$3 million of state-of-the-art tofu manufacturing equipment.

1982

The business expanded and soon run out of operation space. A bigger Factory in Ang Mo Kio was bought over to meet the increasing business demands.

1980

Unicurd began producing hygienically packed tofu in a factory in Aljunied. Unicurd introduces to Singapore the first hygienically packed pasteurized silken tofu, in hygienically sealed boxes and in tubes where was an insant success.
